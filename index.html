Our Contract & Service Policies

Property Management Contract Policy

Our property management agreements are strictly tailored to comply with the Puerto Rico Short Term Rental guidelines and local regulations. We ensure full compliance with the Compañía de Turismo (Puerto Rico Tourism Company) registration, Room Tax collection and remissions, and municipal noise ordinances. Our contract delineates transparent accounting, emergency maintenance protocols, and clear revenue distribution. We require all managed properties to meet safety standards to provide a secure, hazard-free environment for guests, protecting the owner from liability.

Labor Services & Projects Policy

For specific labor projects outside of standard Airbnb management—such as commercial pressure washing, site clearing, and large-scale repairs—FS Property operates under strict Puerto Rico labor guidelines. As a licensed General Contractor, we ensure all assigned vendors and laborers are fully vetted, properly insured, and adhere to local safety regulations. Our labor contracts provide clear scopes of work, defined timelines, and robust liability protections for commercial and residential property owners.
